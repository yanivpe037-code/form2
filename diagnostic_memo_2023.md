## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY — 90-SECOND READ

The Israeli municipal sector posted a **net aggregate deficit of ₪520M in FY2023** (receipts ₪91.5B vs payments ₪92.1B).
**42% of all 258 municipalities (108 entities) are individually in deficit.**
A structural two-tier divide separates tax-rich metropolitan municipalities from chronically government-dependent periphery.
Revenue grew 9.8% nationally but is deeply unequal.

**Overall Sector Health: AMBER-RED**

## TOP 5 INSIGHTS

1. **Structural sector insolvency** — Aggregate sector deficit of -₪520M; 108/258 municipalities in deficit
2. **Two-tier system hardening** — gov_dep ↔ tax_rev correlation -0.79; converging is not happening
3. **Admin crowds out education** — -0.51 correlation; 2 municipalities at 0% education
4. **Rapid-growers need fiscal guardrails** — 13 municipalities grew >20%; spike risk is real
5. **Debt burden compounding in 10 municipalities** — Combined debt service >6% of total payments
